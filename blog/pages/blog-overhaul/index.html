Blog overhaul.

Published: Oct 1, 2023

I had some time this weekend to start a small side project I have been debating doing. It was to update the blog portion of this site, and make it use the the and CSS of the main website. I didn't fully know if I wanted to put in the effort to port it over, but I was a bit bored this morning and waiting for my laundry to finish. After looking at the code, it did not seem like too much of a task to do so but it certainly wasn't an easy or simple thing to do.

I'm very happy with the results however, as now the blog looks much cleaner. That may make me decide to post here more as I'm finally happy with the result, however I'm sure I will change and fix up things as I always do. The site as a whole looks much cleaner, and now that the blog matches, and there are other features which have been implemented (i.e. Dark/Light mode, etc...) I have a much deeper understanding of the flow of the website and what I truly want out of it.
