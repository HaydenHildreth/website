PlantTimer.

Version: 1.0.0

This is a software for tracking when you need to water and care for your plants. It can run as a server, and be viewed remotely on other devices. Either via LAN or WAN connection.

This allows you to view the plants, add, remove and update plants, from multiple devices. The software is multiplatform, so feel free to use it on your favorite operating systems without issue.
